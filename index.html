Bio

I'm Bo Wu (Chinese Name: 吴波), a Research Scientist at Columbia University in New York, working with Prof. Shih-Fu Chang.
I received my Ph.D. at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT,CAS), advised by Prof. Jintao Li, and worked in the group of Prof. Yongdong Zhang. I had my research internships at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) under the supervision of Dr. Tao Mei and Dr. Jianlong Fu. I was visiting PhD student at Academia Sinica under the supervision of Prof. Wen-Huang Cheng.
My research interests are deep learning, multimedia and computer vision.
