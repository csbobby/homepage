Bio

I'm Bo Wu (Chinese Name: 吴波), a Research Scientist at DVMM Lab of Columbia University in New York.

I received my Ph.D. at Institute of Computing Technology,Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT,CAS), advised by Prof. Jintao Li, and worked in the group of Prof. Yongdong Zhang. I have research internships at Microsoft Research Asia and my research mentors are Tao Mei and Jianlong Fu at MSRA. And I was visiting PhD at Academia Sinica and worked with Prof. Wen-Huang Cheng.

My research interests are deep learning, multimedia and computer vision.
